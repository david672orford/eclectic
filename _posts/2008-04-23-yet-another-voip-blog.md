There is already no shortage of IP telephony blogs. Many of them are valuable
and interesting. Many of them are written from the standpoint of a observer
watching the facinating new devices and services which appear on the market.

My blog will take a different direction. It will be written from the standpoint of
a telephony hobbyist. It will be about my attempts to use Asterisk and surplus
equipment to create a telephone system for my family and friends.

I hope to cover such topics as hacking Linksys ATA's and creating a custom Asterisk
dialplan from scratch.

IP telephony has been my hobby for two years now. For a
year now it is also part of my work at a small college in New England. So, I
may tell about our experience intergrating Asterisk with a Nortel Meridian
phone switch.
