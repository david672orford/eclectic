How to get around Connection Reset on DV Lottery application:

Increase the JPEG
compression on your photos until they are 60KB or smaller. Keep the dimensions
at the required 600 by 600 pixels.

If you still get Connection Reset, don't
despair, you don't need to enter everything again:

* Start a new application in a separate window or tab
* Do the Captcha
* Go back to your original application and reload the page
